General Questions

Basic information about Infinite Protection , what it does, and who it's built for.

Infinite Protection is an all-in-one cybersecurity solution offering 24/7 VPN encryption, virus and malware protection, and internet controls — all managed from a single dashboard. It's designed to protect families, small businesses, and remote teams without requiring any technical expertise.

Infinite Protection is ideal for small and medium-sized businesses, families, ministries, nonprofits, and remote or hybrid teams. If you need consistent, easy-to-manage cybersecurity across multiple devices and users — without an IT department — Infinite Protection was built for you.

Infinite Protection protects proactively so you never find out about a breach the hard way.

Unlike other VPNs that only activate on demand, Infinite Protection 's VPN is always on — the moment you turn on your device, you're protected. The same 256-bit AES encryption used by the military and banks shields your data at all times.

A Virtual Private Network (VPN) creates a secure, encrypted tunnel around your internet traffic so that no one — not hackers, governments, or even your Internet Service Provider — can see what you're sending or receiving online.

A VPN is necessary because tools to intercept your data have become increasingly sophisticated. Without one, sensitive passwords, identity details, and financial information can all be exposed, leading to compromised accounts, identity theft, and worse.

Product

Product & Features

Learn about the core tools and security features included in Infinite Protection .

Infinite Protection covers three key areas of online security: a 24/7 VPN that encrypts all your traffic, virus and malware protection that blocks ransomware, spyware, adware, and phishing attempts in real-time, and internet controls that let administrators filter content categories and monitor all device activity from a central dashboard.

Yes. Infinite Protection is available on almost every device — Windows, macOS, Android, Apple iOS (phones and tablets), Kindle Fire, and in beta on Chromebook. You can protect an unlimited number of devices, all managed from one account dashboard.

Infinite Protection is engineered for performance. The app is extremely lightweight — only 12MB on Windows, 30MB on macOS, 11MB on iOS, and 30MB on Android. Most users notice no meaningful difference in browsing speed, and Infinite Protection works seamlessly on both WiFi and cellular networks anywhere in the world.

Malware is an umbrella term for unwanted software that can harm your device or compromise your accounts. Infinite Protection protects against Ransomware (locks your files and demands payment), Trojans (hide in downloads and become active once installed), Adware (unwanted advertising that can slow devices and signal deeper compromise), Spyware, and Phishing attempts.

Entourage shows you all the background activity happening when you visit a website or use an app — including third-party trackers, advertising networks, and other unsolicited connections that "tag along" with legitimate traffic. Infinite Protection filters the unwanted Entourage and gives you a complete, transparent view of what's being allowed into your network and what's being blocked.

With an internet filter, an administrator can block entire content categories from accessing a network or device — for example, blocking pornography, social media, or specific websites during certain hours. Infinite Protection 's dashboard lets you customize these controls per user profile, making it easy to set appropriate access levels for employees, children, or any user group.

A profile is an individual user's account within Infinite Protection that can be customized with its own specific internet control settings and security levels. "Unlimited profiles" means you can create as many as you need to fit the requirements of your organization or family — there's no cap on users.

Then download the Infinite Protection app onto each device, sign in with your credentials, and assign the device to the appropriate profile. Once done for all devices, your setup is complete.

Security

Security & Privacy

Understand how Infinite Protection protects your data, devices, and online activity.

Infinite Protection VPN encrypts all your traffic using OpenVPN and an AES-256 cipher — the same encryption standard used by the military and banks. Any entity looking in from the outside would see only unreadable, scrambled data. Because your private information is fed to the internet in this encrypted state, it remains secure and completely private.

In the interest of user privacy, Infinite Protection only records a limited amount of activity based on the number of entries rather than time. For an individual who spends a regular amount of time online, a Infinite Protection profile shows approximately 1–2 weeks of history — after which the data is automatically deleted. Infinite Protection does not store your information long-term and never sells your personal data — ever.

Yes — and this is one of Infinite Protection 's most important use cases. On any public or private Wi-Fi network, anyone on that network with the right software can potentially look into your device. Infinite Protection 's always-on VPN ensures your connection is private and secured at all times, even on untrusted public networks like coffee shops, airports, or hotels.

Infinite Protection is encrypted with AES-256. A brute force attack would need to try 2²⁵⁵ possible keys — measured in time, this would take approximately 2,117.8 trillion years on average. Beyond encryption, Infinite Protection 's cybersecurity backend is in a near-constant state of configuration to ensure users are fully protected against the threats of today and tomorrow.

All VPNs are not alike. Infinite Protection uses 256-bit encryption — the highest level available — and unlike most VPNs which are "on-demand" (only active when you manually turn them on), Infinite Protection is always on from the moment you turn on your device. A single unprotected minute on public Wi-Fi is all it takes for your safety to be compromised. Infinite Protection closes that gap permanently.

Billing & Trial

Everything you need to know about pricing, the free trial, and cancellation.

Everyone gets a free trial to try Infinite Protection with full access to every feature. You will enter your credit card upon signup, but it will not be billed until after the trial period ends. To cancel before your trial is over, simply go to the hamburger menu on your dashboard and click "Billing Information" — no need to call anyone. Your subscription is completely in your own control.

There is no setup fee and no cancellation penalty. You can cancel at any time directly from your account settings — no phone calls, no questions asked. Infinite Protection is designed to earn your continued trust, not lock you in.

Additional devices are just $2.99/month each. You can add as many as you need directly from your dashboard — there's no cap on device count, and every device gets the exact same level of protection regardless of how many you add.

Yes. Infinite Protection is easy to remove from any device if you decide to cancel. Simply uninstall it like any other app on your device. If you lost or broke a device and can't log out locally, log into your account dashboard online, navigate to that user's profile, and disconnect and delete the old device from the Monitor tab.
