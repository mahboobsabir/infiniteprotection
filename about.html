Our Story

How Infinite Protection Came to Be

Infinite Protection was founded by a group of international entrepreneurs who experienced a cyber catastrophe firsthand. They quickly realized cybersecurity had become the weakest link in their business.

That experience sparked a mission to create powerful cybersecurity built specifically for small and mid-size businesses — protection that is simple to use, affordable, and designed for real-world business needs.

Today, Infinite Protection is an international team of innovators and cybersecurity professionals, headquartered in Colorado, with research and development teams across the United States and Europe.
