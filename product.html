SaferNet

Modern Device Protection. Simple and Unified.

In the past, small businesses used multiple tools — VPNs for secure connections, antivirus for malware, and separate controls for user activity. It was complex, expensive, and left gaps attackers could exploit. SaferNet brings it all together in one cloud-based solution.

  • Built-in VPN encrypts every connection
  • Advanced threat protection blocks malware, ransomware, and phishing
  • Internet controls provide visibility and reduce risky behavior in real time

SaferNet protects every device, anywhere your team works, with one simple unified system.

ZED

Modern Penetration Testing. Faster. More Affordable.

In the past, companies hired white-hat hackers to test websites, software, and networks for weaknesses — often taking months and costing tens of thousands of dollars. ZED delivers the same concept through advanced automation.

  • External testing finds open doors from the outside
  • Internal testing shows what happens if someone gets inside
  • Basic scans identify common and critical risks
  • Complete scans uncover deeper vulnerabilities
  • Compliance testing: SOC 2, ISO 27001, PCI DSS, HIPAA

What once took months now takes minutes or hours — and can be repeated regularly.
