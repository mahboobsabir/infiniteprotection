The Platform

What is Zero Exposure Defense (ZED)?

Zero Exposure Defense (ZED) is a modern penetration testing and vulnerability scanning platform designed to continuously identify risks across your business.

ZED scans your websites, software, networks, and digital assets to find vulnerabilities before attackers can exploit them. Instead of relying on one-time testing or reactive security tools, ZED continuously evaluates your environment and strengthens your security posture over time.

This proactive approach helps reduce exposure, identify weaknesses early, and prevent security incidents before they happen.

Scan Types

Types of Security Scans

ZED uses multiple types of scans to identify vulnerabilities and reduce your risk across your entire business.

External Scanning

OUTSIDE-IN

Identifies vulnerabilities that attackers can see from the internet, including exposed services, open ports, and outdated software.

Internal Scanning

INSIDE-OUT

Identifies risks inside your network, showing what could happen if an attacker gains access to your systems.

Basic Scans

ESSENTIAL

Identify common and critical vulnerabilities that create immediate risk and need to be addressed first.

Complete Scans

ADVANCED

Uncover deeper and more advanced vulnerabilities across your entire environment for comprehensive coverage.

Together, these scans provide a complete view of your security and help reduce your overall attack surface.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What does ZED scan?

ZED scans your websites, software, networks, and digital assets to identify vulnerabilities and security risks across your entire business environment.

How often does ZED run scans?

ZED runs continuous scanning and can perform scheduled scans regularly to identify new vulnerabilities as they appear in your environment.

Is ZED the same as penetration testing?

ZED provides modern, automated penetration testing combined with continuous vulnerability scanning for ongoing protection — faster and more affordable than traditional testing.

Does ZED help with compliance?

Yes. ZED supports compliance efforts including SOC 2, ISO 27001, PCI DSS, and HIPAA by identifying security gaps and risks that could impact your compliance readiness.

Is ZED built for small businesses?

Yes. ZED is designed specifically for small and mid-size businesses that need strong security without enterprise complexity or enterprise pricing.

How does ZED work with SaferNet?

SaferNet protects your devices while ZED identifies vulnerabilities. Together they form Infinite Protection — two pillars that secure both how attackers get in and where they try to break in.
