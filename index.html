Penetration Testing Explained

What's included in every scan?

All basic and full scans are classified as penetration tests. Our team simulates real-world attacks to expose vulnerabilities before bad actors find them.

🔍
Basic Scan

Quick Vulnerability Scan

A targeted penetration test focused on the most commonly vulnerable entry points. Ideal for routine security checks and baseline threat assessment.

🔬
Full Scan

Comprehensive Evaluation

A deep-dive penetration test covering all aspects of a website or software system — leaving no potential exploit unexamined.

🌐
External Scan

Outside-In Assessment

Evaluates your defenses from an attacker's perspective. Identifies where a hacker could gain access or cause damage from the public internet.

🏢
Internal Scan

Inside-Out Risk Analysis

Assesses risks from within your environment — evaluating the potential damage if a breach or insider threat occurs inside your network.

Compliance Scans — Add-On

Add compliance-specific penetration testing to any package. We map findings directly to your required framework controls.
