What happens during the 30-day free trial?

You get full access to all features in your selected plan. No credit card required, and you can cancel anytime. There are no obligations — the trial is completely free for 30 days, after which you can choose to subscribe or walk away with no charges.

Can I switch plans later?

Absolutely. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from your account dashboard. Upgrades take effect immediately; downgrades take effect at the start of your next billing cycle. Your data is never lost during a plan switch.

What if I need more than one device?

Our plans are priced per endpoint, so you simply add more devices to your plan as needed. Whether you have 5 or 500 endpoints, pricing scales linearly with no surprise fees. Volume discounts are available for larger deployments.

Is there a setup fee or cancellation penalty?

No setup fees and no cancellation penalties — ever. We believe in earning your trust through results, not lock-in contracts. You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings with just a few clicks.

Do you offer discounts for larger teams?

Yes! We offer tiered volume discounts starting at 10 endpoints. Teams of 50+ endpoints qualify for custom enterprise pricing, which includes dedicated account management, priority support SLAs, and tailored compliance reporting. Contact our sales team for a quote.
