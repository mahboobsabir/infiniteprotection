PRICING FAQ

Pricing questions

Simple, transparent answers to common questions about our pricing and plans.

What happens during the 10-day free trial?

You get full access to the Infinite Protection platform with no credit card charge during the trial period. You can explore the dashboard, device protection features, and select scanning functionality before your subscription begins.

When will my card be charged?

Your card is not charged until the 10-day free trial ends. If you cancel before the trial expires, you will not be billed.

What happens after the free trial ends?

Your selected subscription automatically begins after the trial period unless canceled beforehand.

Can I switch plans later?

Yes. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Plan upgrades take effect immediately with prorated billing adjustments. Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.

What happens to my scans if I switch plans?

Unused scan credits transfer when upgrading plans. If downgrading, your new scan limits begin at the next renewal period.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes. You can cancel anytime directly through your account or by contacting support.

Is there a setup fee or cancellation penalty?

No setup fees or cancellation penalties. However, completed scans, assessments, and compliance services are non-refundable once delivered because they create immediate operational and processing costs.

Do you offer refunds?

If no scans or compliance assessments have been used, customers may request a full refund within the first 30 days after billing begins. If scans or compliance services have already been completed, those services are non-refundable and may be deducted from any refund amount.

What if I need more than one device?

Every plan includes device protection through SaferNet, and additional devices can easily be added at any time.

Do you offer discounts for larger teams?

Yes. We offer volume discounts for growing businesses and larger teams. Contact our team for custom pricing options for organizations with larger deployments.

What happens if I do not use all my scans?

Unused scans expire at the end of your annual subscription term. Customers can schedule scans anytime during the active subscription period.

Can I purchase additional scans if needed?

Yes. Additional scans and compliance assessments can be purchased individually at discounted subscriber pricing.

Are scans automatic or scheduled?

Some monitoring and protection features run continuously, while advanced internal scans and compliance assessments can be scheduled at times that work best for your business.

Will scans disrupt my business or network?

Infinite Protection scanning is designed to minimize operational disruption while safely identifying vulnerabilities across your environment.

Do I need technical expertise to use Infinite Protection?

No. Infinite Protection was designed specifically for small and mid-size businesses with a simple dashboard, guided reporting, and easy-to-understand recommendations.

Can Infinite Protection protect remote employees?

Yes. SaferNet protects devices anywhere your employees work, including remote offices, public Wi-Fi, travel, and hybrid work environments.

What types of scans are included in my plan?

Depending on your plan, Infinite Protection may include:
• External vulnerability scans
• Internal vulnerability scans
• Advanced penetration testing
• Compliance assessments
• Continuous monitoring

What compliance frameworks do you support?

Infinite Protection currently supports:
• PCI DSS
• HIPAA
• SOC 2
• ISO 27001

Can I schedule scans around business hours?

Yes. Advanced scans can be scheduled during maintenance windows or lower-traffic periods to minimize disruption.

Is my data secure during scanning?

Yes. All scans, reports, and customer data are protected using encrypted communication and secure cloud infrastructure.

How quickly do I receive scan results?

Most scans generate results quickly, while deeper assessments and compliance reviews may take additional processing time depending on complexity.

Can Infinite Protection work alongside our current IT provider or MSP?

Yes. Infinite Protection is designed to work alongside internal IT teams, managed service providers, and outside security consultants.

What makes Infinite Protection different?

Infinite Protection combines device security, vulnerability scanning, compliance tools, malware defense, content filtering, and always-on protection into one unified platform designed specifically for small and mid-size businesses.
